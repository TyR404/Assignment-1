PlayStation (officially abbreviated as PS) is a video game brand that is produced by Sony Interactive Entertainment, a division of Sony. PlayStation has released a large variety of products. These products consist of five home video game consoles, two handheld video game consoles, a media center, a smartphone, as well as an online service and multiple magazines.


Since the initial release of the original PlayStation in 1994, there have been 111 million registered users and approximately 576.1 million consoles sold.
